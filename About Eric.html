Early Life

Raised in the Mount Prospect suburb of Illinois, Eric grew up with his brother Mark, his father Ken and his mother Jeanna. His brother is 2 years older than him, and Eric and him grew up very close. So close that Eric starred in one of Mark's parody rap videos when they were in high school. To this day, the two are best friends.

Career

Upon graduating from Northwestern University, Eric scored a spot in a local company Leviton's rotation program. During the program, we would live in a U.S. city for 6 months before moving to a new one and staying there for 6 months and moving and staying again ad hoc. The program has allowed him to live in Illinois, North Carolina and now, Seattle. Eric currently works as a Manufacturing Engineer at Leviton, based in Bothell, WA. He works in network solutions.
